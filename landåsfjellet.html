Walks and hikes on Landåsfjellet

Landåsfjellet is next to Ulriken and is located in Bergen, Hordaland, Norway. Enjoy this 4.3-km circular trail near Bergen, Vestland. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 1 h 38 min to complete. This is a very popular area for hiking, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring. Dogs are welcome and may be off-lead in some areas.


A nice and family-friendly tour with a great 360 degree panoramic view of Bergen and the surrounding area! Landåsfjellet is a mountain in Bergen municipality in Western Norway that sits at 428 meters above sea level. The trip is easy and alternates between gravel road and clear path through the forest and open terrain. Along the way you walk along a nice little river, and past a gapahuk called "Eplehuset". At the highest point there is a cairn in concrete. Here you have a great view in all directions.

Postcard Beauty

With stunning views, Landåsfjellet is a popular hiking area in Bergen with hiking trails up from both Nattlandsfjellet and Sædalen as well as paths from several sides. The area is used a lot all year round. Take in the amazing sights.
